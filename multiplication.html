Multiplication

Multiplication is a way to increase a number over and over. Multiplying is a faster way of adding more then one thing for example if you had 4 sweets + 4 sweets + 4 sweets you could just multiply 4 times 3 to get the same answer

4+4 = 8 + 4 = 12 and 4x3 = 12

You can use this picture to help you understand

The x sign symbolises multiplication in equations so Five times Five is written as 5x5
