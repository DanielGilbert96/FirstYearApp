Division

Division is a way of splitting things equally.

Take this picture for example

Lets say you have 10 tickets and 5 friends and you want to divide them out evenly

We divide them out evenly so that they have 2 each so therefore 10÷2 = 5

The ÷ sign symbolises division in equations so Five divided by Five is written as 5÷5
