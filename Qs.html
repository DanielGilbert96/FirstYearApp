Question 3

This ones a toughy!

A Train travels at 60km/ph, if the journey to mayo is 150km how long would it take for the train to get there?

If the Train took 2 hours to do the same journey what speed was it traveling at?

If the Train was traveling at 80km/ph for 3 hours how long did it travel?

Tip * Time = Distance/Speed and Speed = Distance/time and Distance = SpeedxTime

(i)D/S 150/60 = 2.5 = 2 hours 30 mins (ii)D/T 150/2 = 75km/ph (iii)SxT 80x3 = 240km
